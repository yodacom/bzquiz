Standard Reference Method (SRM)

SRM is a scale for measuring the color intensity of a beer SRM was adopted by the Ameican Society of Brewing Chemists in 1951. There is another, older scale named degrees Lovibond (devised by Joseph Williams Lovibond) that the SRM scale has replaced.

The color chart and colors below are an approximation of the actual colors in the SRM scale. Choose one of these colors to match the beer you are drinking
